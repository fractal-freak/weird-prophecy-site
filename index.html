About the Astrologer

Rigorous tradition,
practical application

For years I've studied the oldest systematic methods of chart interpretation, the techniques of Valens, Dorotheus, and the Hellenistic masters who built astrology's foundational logic. My work is research-driven and historically grounded. When I read a chart, I'm drawing from the same tradition that once advised kings and timed the founding of cities.

Rather than dealing in vague affirmations, I offer concrete predictions with specific timing, the kind of astrology that can actually be tested and measured. Every reading is rooted in technique, backed by research, and delivered with the clarity you need to make real decisions.

When I'm not reading charts, I'm producing research content for my YouTube channel and Substack, correlating daily world events with planetary transits, and building tools that push the practice forward.
